MARKETING AT SACHACK SacHack is Sacramento’s first intercollegiate hackathon developed by a team of students from UC Davis and Sacramento State University. The first event took place in McClellan Conference Center in Sacramento on November 2018. The event was co-hosted by the Sacramento Kings as a way to promote innovations and new data solutions in the Sacramento area. As the Director of Marketing for SacHacks, my task was to gain interest within colleges and universities across America and spread the word of the event.

With SacHacks being a new hackathon, my team and I needed to start from scratch and utilize social media to reach a greater audience. In order to promote SacHacks, I have contacted computer science, design, and game development departments in most of the community colleges and universities in California and create partnerships with established hackathon communities. Our outreach strategy soon capture the attention of not just colleges across the United States but other countries as well, including India and Canada. Within three months, the number of registrations more than tripled from thirty applicants to over a thousand.

MARKETING AT ASCEND UC DAVIS Ascend is the largest, non-profit Pan-Asian organization for business professionals in North America. Ascend UC Davis is a college chapter of this organization. We provide opportunities for students to expand their network, gain business skills, and learn from professionals.

I joined Ascend UC Davis during the fall of 2017, hoping to learn more about business and marketing. As a Cinema and Digital Media major, I was looking to combine my experience in film and digital art to market a product through media. I soon joined the marketing team as a Marketing Committee member.

As a Marketing Committee member, I created Ascend’s first major promotional video. This video features guest speakers, socials, and general meetings. The idea behind the creation of this video was to engage our audience in a visually appealing way and to show all the benefits that Ascend has to offer. Additionally, I photographed Ascend meetings and events for social media and raised awareness of our organization through classroom announcements and tabling on campus.

As of June 2018, I became the Vice President of Marketing at Ascend UC Davis. As the marketing officer, I manage Ascend’s Facebook page and Instagram account. I continually promote events on Facebook in order to keep our members updated on different opportunities and events. Additionally, I post weekly on Ascend’s Instagram in order to increase our exposure to the public and create a greater interest within our community. I also create flyers and Facebook events for upcoming affairs.
